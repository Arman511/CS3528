Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and store your data while using our platform.

We use cookies solely for the purpose of maintaining your session while you are logged into the platform. These cookies are used to identify whether you are logged in and to provide a personalized experience. We do not store any personal information other than what is necessary for the operation of our platform. Specifically, we store a session cookie to track your login status.

Data We Collect

We collect basic information such as your email address, first name, last name, and other details necessary to create and maintain your account. This data is used for login authentication and personalization of your experience on the platform.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the purpose of authenticating you when you log in, managing your account, and providing a personalized experience. We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

Changes to This Policy
