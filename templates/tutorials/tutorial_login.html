Login Tutorial

To login, click on the "Login" on the question "Are you part of the placement team? Login" on the student/employer login page.
Once you click on the "Login" button, you will be redirected to the login for placement teams.
Enter your username and password and click on the "Login" button. This will redirect you to the admin dashboard.
If you want to sign out of the admin dashboard, click on the dropdown in the top right corner and press signout.
