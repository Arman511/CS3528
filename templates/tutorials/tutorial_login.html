Placement Team

Either go to the Placement team login page or click the Placement team login button in the landing page or press the "Are you part of the placement team? Login" link on the other login pages. You will be asked to enter your email and password and then press submit and you will login. If you have forgotten your password, asking your admin to reset it for you.

Enter login information in the red boxes highlighted below, then pressing the login button.

Account creation is handled by the admin team. If you are a new member of the placement team, please contact the admin team to create an account for you.

Student

The university placement team manage all student accounts. They will create an account on your behalf.

Once you have been notified by the university that your account has been created, at the student login section of the webpage please enter your university student identifier number in the corresponding input box.

To log in, enter your account information in the red highlighted boxes, then selecting the login button.

After which a unique numerical 'one-time passcode' will be sent to your student email address.

A card modal will then appear, with an input box where you enter the code.

If a code has not arrived to your email firstly, please check your spam folder, if not please contact a member of the placement team.

Employer

The university placement team manage all employer accounts. They will create an account on your behalf.

Once you have been notified by the university that your account has been created, at the employer login section of the webpage please enter your email in the corresponding input box.

After which a unique numerical 'one-time passcode' will be sent to your employer email address.

A modal will then appear, with an input box where you enter the code.

If a code has not arrived to your email firstly, please check your spam folder, if not please contact a member of the placement team.
